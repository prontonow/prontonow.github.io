About Pronto

Pronto is a free-to-use mobile application that connects college students who are looking to make money to potential job listings in their area through geolocation. Users can interact with a visual map of their area to see jobs around them in various industries and complete them for competitively adapted prices.

App Launching November 2017

Mission Statement

We strive to connect people who need jobs done to people who will do them

All Features

  • Post and Request Jobs
  • Rate Freelancers and Employers
  • Search for Relevant Tasks near them

Why Pronto?

Unlike many other freelancing services, Pronto lets users directly interact with their regional economy through an interactive map. Pronto also focuses on universality by allowing the market to be driven by competitively priced tasks, rather than having a market focused on the services that freelancers can offer. This unique service model allows for any user to become a freelancer and earn extra income as college students.
